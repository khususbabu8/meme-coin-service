FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to provide?
Just your token idea — name, ticker, concept, and logo (if you have one). For Standard/Premium, you'll also need SOL for the liquidity pool. We handle all the technical work.
How does the LP (Liquidity Pool) work?
You send SOL for the LP (minimum 2-5 SOL recommended). We create the trading pair on Raydium and lock the LP tokens so nobody can withdraw the liquidity. This proves your token isn't a rug pull.
Is my money safe during the process?
We use milestone-based payment. Service fee upfront, LP funds are used specifically for LP creation. You can verify everything on-chain — LP lock, token deployment, all transparent on Solana Explorer.
How long does delivery take?
Basic: 24 hours. Standard: 24-48 hours. Premium: 48 hours. We'll give you an exact timeline when you order.
Can I customize the smart contract?
Yes! Premium package includes custom features like transaction tax, auto-burn, holder rewards, etc. Basic and Standard use standard SPL token with mint/freeze revoked.
Do you guarantee the token will be successful?
No. We handle the technical side — deployment, LP, website. Success depends on your marketing, community building, and market conditions. We provide guidance but can't guarantee results.
What chains do you support?
Primary: Solana. We also support Base (Ethereum L2), BSC, and Ethereum mainnet. Multi-chain deployment available for Premium clients.
